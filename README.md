## 🏗️ Project Portfolio Index

This repository contains **8 distinct project modules**, each with its own standalone logic and interface.

### 🏠 [Main Hub Dashboard](./index.html)
The central command center for the entire suite, featuring GSAP cinematic reveals and a dynamic neural background.

### 🛡️ [1. Industrial Safety Monitor](./industrial-safety/index.html)
- **Sector**: Manufacturing & Construction
- **AI Core**: TensorFlow.js (COCO-SSD)
- **Feature**: Real-time PPE and hazard identification within industrial zones.

### 📈 [2. Quantum Pulse Financial AI](./quantum-pulse/index.html)
- **Sector**: Fintech & Market Analysis
- **AI Core**: Predictive Sequence Modeling
- **Feature**: Market trend forecasting with high-precision Chart.js dashboards.

### 🧬 [3. Kinetic Health Monitor](./kinetic-health/index.html)
- **Sector**: Healthcare & Physical Therapy
- **AI Core**: PoseNet (Skeletal Estimation)
- **Feature**: Clinical postural and joint-tracking for motion-based recovery.

### 🛒 [4. Eco-Commerce AI](./eco-commerce/index.html)
- **Sector**: Retail & Consumer Goods
- **AI Core**: Smart Value Analysis Engine
- **Feature**: Dynamic pricing and visual recommendation models for modern shopping.

### 🏛️ [5. Enterprise Cognitive Hub](./enterprise-hub/index.html)
- **Sector**: Corporate & Knowledge Management
- **AI Core**: NLP Pattern Matching
- **Feature**: Autonomous internal query handling and strategic business intelligence.

### 📐 [6. Generative Design Studio](./gen-design/index.html)
- **Sector**: Engineering & CAD
- **AI Core**: Autonomous Diffusion Prototyping
- **Feature**: Generative industrial blueprints and functional design conceptualization.

### 📊 [7. Brand Perception Analytics](./brand-perception/index.html)
- **Sector**: Marketing & PR
- **AI Core**: Semantic Sentiment Engine
- **Feature**: Real-time emotional clustering of social metadata for brand intelligence.

### 📰 [8. Nexus Intelligence Blog](./nexus-blog/index.html)
- **Sector**: Digital Content & Media
- **AI Core**: Cognitive Text Summarizer
- **Feature**: Automated semantic summaries and sentiment-driven content ingestion.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **AI Frameworks**: TensorFlow.js, PoseNet, COCO-SSD
- **Data Visualization**: Chart.js 4.0
- **Interaction**: GSAP 3.0 (Smooth Motion & Reveal System)
- **Design System**: Vanilla CSS3 (Glassmorphism Core), FontAwesome 6.4
- **Edge Architecture**: 100% Client-side model inference (Zero latency, High privacy)
